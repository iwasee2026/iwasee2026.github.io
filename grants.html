Grants Program

Students & Early-Career Scientists

About

IWASEE aims to cultivate and enhance expertise in advanced systems efficiency engineering. We are pleased to announce the launch of a grant program designed to support students and early-career scientists attending IWASEE 2026. These grants can be used to fund:

  • Registration fees for the workshop.
  • Partially or fully travel and accommodation expenses.

Eligibility

Grants will be awarded strongly emphasizing diversity, equity, and inclusion. Special consideration will be given to applicants from underrepresented groups, including those of diverse genders, indigenous backgrounds, and regions with limited access to opportunities.

In line with the workshop's mission, we strongly encourage talented graduate students and early-career scientists to apply and seize this opportunity to further their expertise and contribute to the advancement of efficient and sustainable engineering systems.

Applications Procedure

Interested participants can apply by completing the Grant Application Form (Coming soon), where you will submit the following documents in a single PDF file:

  • A CV.
  • A one-page statement outlining your research background, interests, and motivation for attending IWASEE 2026.
  • A letter of support from a professor or advisor confirming that the requested funding (for travel, accommodation, and/or a percentage of the registration fee) is unavailable through other sources.
