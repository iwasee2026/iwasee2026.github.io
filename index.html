Welcome to AMW 2026

The Alberto Mendelzon International Workshop (AMW) is oriented to discuss and spread research results in the areas of Foundations of Databases and the Web.

Our goal is to facilitate research exchanges with the Latin American community. AMW is a vitally relevant academic event where students and young researchers can meet top researchers from all over the world, promoting collaboration, learning, and teaching in a friendly environment.
