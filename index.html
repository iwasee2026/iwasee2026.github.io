About The Event

Welcome to IWASEE 2026

IWASEE brings together researchers, engineers, students, and IEEE professionals to promote collaboration and scientific exchange in emerging technologies.

Hosted by the KR - Knowledge Representation Research Group at Universidad Católica San Pablo, IWASEE aims to strengthen international collaboration and interdisciplinary innovation to build smarter, sustainable, safe and efficient systems for the future.
