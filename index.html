Welcome to IWASEE 2026

IWASEE brings together researchers, engineers, students, and IEEE professionals to promote collaboration and scientific exchange in emerging technologies that contribute to smarter, more efficient systems for society and industry.

Hosted by the KR - Knowledge Representation Research Group at Universidad Católica San Pablo, IWASEE aims to strengthen international collaboration and interdisciplinary innovation.
