About the IWASEE Workshop

About IWASEE

The International Workshop on Advanced Systems Efficiency Engineering (IWASEE 2026) is an international forum for discussing innovative technologies and research advances in efficient, intelligent, and sustainable engineering systems.

IWASEE brings together researchers, engineers, students, and IEEE professionals to promote collaboration and scientific exchange in emerging technologies that contribute to smarter, more efficient systems for society and industry. Modern technological systems must be intelligent, connected, energy-efficient, and sustainable. IWASEE was created to provide an international platform where researchers and professionals can discuss solutions for next-generation systems involving efficient computing, autonomous technologies, wireless communication, and smart connected environments.

Hosted by the KR - Knowledge Representation Research Group at Universidad Católica San Pablo, the workshop aims to strengthen international collaboration and interdisciplinary innovation capable of generating scientific, industrial, and social impact.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To promote international collaboration, scientific research, and technological innovation in efficient engineering systems, robotics, automation, wireless technologies, IoT applications and Electromagnetic Compatibility.

Vision

To become a recognized international workshop in advanced engineering technologies, connecting academia, industry, and society to build smarter, sustainable, safe and efficient systems for the future.
