Call for Papers

IWASEE 2026 invites researchers, practitioners, and students to submit high-quality, original contributions covering the latest advances in efficient, intelligent, and sustainable engineering systems.

  • Track 1: Green Computing and Efficient Systems
  • Green AI and Machine Learning
  • Algorithmic Resource Optimization
  • Efficient Data Management and Information Systems

  • Track 2: Robotics, Automation, and Control

  • Control systems
  • Autonomous systems
  • Smart manufacturing
  • Track 3: Wireless Systems, Antennas, and IoT
  • Antenna design
  • Wireless communications
  • 5G/6G

  • Track 4: Green Computing and Sustainable Infrastructures
  • Energy-efficient computing

  • Cloud / Edge efficiency
  • Software Engineering for Green Systems

Submission Rules

Papers must be submitted in English. Please follow the standard IEEE conference formatting guidelines (two-column format) for your initial submission.

At least one author of each accepted paper must attend the workshop to present the work.

We invite submissions of original research covering the four strategic tracks of IWASEE 2026:

  • Track 1: Green Computing and Efficient Systems
  • Track 2: Robotics, Automation, and Control
  • Track 3: Wireless Systems, Antennas, and IoT
  • Track 4: Green Computing and Sustainable Infrastructures

Submissions will be judged by the Program Committee based on scientific quality, relevance, and originality. All papers must be submitted electronically via the official conference submission portal in PDF format. Authors are encouraged to ensure their work contributes to the advancement of efficient, intelligent, and sustainable engineering systems.

Camera-Ready Guidelines

  1. Ensure your final manuscript adheres to the official IEEE conference proceedings format.
  2. Verify that all author names, affiliations, and contact details are accurately reflected in the final document.
  3. Include the appropriate copyright clearance code notice at the bottom of the first page, if required by IEEE guidelines.
  4. Remove all page numbers and running headers from the final PDF.
  5. Check for any missing fonts or broken links within the document.
  6. Prepare all source files (LaTeX or Word) along with the final PDF for submission.
  7. The camera-ready version must be uploaded to the submission portal by June 30th, 2026.

Your camera-ready package should include:

  • Final PDF of the paper.
  • Compressed file (ZIP) containing all source files (LaTeX files with .bib, or Word document).
  • Signed copyright transfer form (instructions will be provided upon acceptance).
