3MPC Competition

Three Minute Presentation Challenge

Step into the spotlight and pitch your ideas, research, or innovations to a panel of non-specialist judges using just one static slide. You have exactly 3 minutes to make it engaging, accessible, and unforgettable.

Presentation Categories

English Category

Present your original research, ideas, or applications clearly communicating complex concepts to a non-specialist audience.

Spanish Category

Present a topic of your choice in Spanish, delivering impactful content for a non-technical audience.

Important Dates

  • Extended Deadline May 21 — May 23 Video Submission Deadline
  • Live Event May 25 Grand Finale!

Who can participate?

Open to Undergraduate, Graduate, and PhD students.

Judging Criteria

  • Clarity for non-technical audience
  • Storytelling & structure
  • Overall impact and engagement
